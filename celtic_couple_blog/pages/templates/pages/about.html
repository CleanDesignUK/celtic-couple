Welcome to Our World

We are Dave and Georgina Roberts, living near the outskirts of Snowdonia National Park in North West Wales. By day, Dave works in the hospital, proudly serving the local community, while Georgina is an avid creative, capturing life’s best moments on camera. Our shared passion for travel, Celtic heritage, and exploring new places inspired us to start this blog.

From scenic countryside walks to big city excursions, we hope our adventures spark your curiosity and encourage you to pack your bags for your own unforgettable journey.

Our Story

A Chance Encounter

We first met in 2007 in a café, chatted over a cup of coffee, then went our separate ways—only to meet again the very next day on the M56! We both recognised each other, pulled over into the next layby, and that was the magical start of our story. From that point on, life has been one scenic ride after another.

Since that moment, we’ve geared our lifestyle around cycling, walking, and travelling—mostly in our beloved Volkswagen campervan. Together, we’ve visited various corners of Europe and many islands of Scotland, including the Outer Hebrides, Orkney, and Shetland, all while diving headfirst into local cultures and breathtaking natural beauty.

Celebrating Celtic Heritage

Proudly Welsh & Cornish

Dave’s Welsh background and Georgina’s Cornish roots create a unique blend of Celtic traditions. We find joy in discovering historical landmarks, connecting with local cultures, and indulging in local delicacies—like a hearty Welsh rarebit or a traditional Cornish pasty.

Every year, we set out to uncover new routes, ports, boat events, car shows, and festivals, all while taking in the wonderful sights, sounds, and tastes along the way.

Sharing Celtic pride wherever we go

Join Us on This Journey

We invite you to explore our blog and follow our adventures around the UK and Europe. From rugged coastlines to hidden countryside paths, each trip is a chance to discover something new about the world—and ourselves.
